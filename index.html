About Her

    SIGNS OF POWER is Dublin based sign painter Vanessa Power who specialises in hand lettered type, hand painted signs and murals. Her bold typography conveys a contemporary and timeless urban aesthetic. And with her love for clean, well executed type, she aims to apply this to all her work. Her vibrant style acts as a bridge between the traditional craft of sign painting and the arresting aesthetic of the pop art movement. Attracting commissions for Jameson, The Guinness Storehouse, the Devlin Hotel, as well as Facebook and Twitter and a myriad of national and international brands.

    ‘THREESOME’ Solo Exhibition

    Thursday October 3rd sees the launch of Threesome, by Signs of Power at Hang Tough Gallery in Portobello, Dublin 8. Vanessa Power, under the pseudonym of Signs of Power, has a unique and arresting approach to sign painting that is synonymous to anyone who knows Dublin. Power’s bold typography and lettering convey a contemporary and timeless urban aesthetic..Here above are some examples from the exhibition.

    Hang Tough Gallery will be showcasing 10 new artworks from Vanessa with her latest solo exhibition – Threesome. Following on from previous shows in Chicago, Belfast, London and Dublin, Threesome demonstrates a further evolution of Power’s art, for the first time combining her renowned typographical approach with gold leaf and everyday objects.
